#showme

Syntax: #showme [{color }] {<text>}

This command will display <text> on your screen by the specified color. Color isnt obligatory parametr. #showme may be used in both forms:

  • #showme This is just text displayed by the defualt color
  • #showme {red} {this text will be displayed buy red color}

Colors specifications are same as in the #highlight command

Example:

#action{%0 decays in your hands} {#showme {*** %0 decayed !!!}}

Now, you will see a message "sword decayed !!!" every time a sword decays in your hands.

See also: #highlight #output
