#tickset

Syntax: #tickset

Resets the tickcounter. It's good to synchronise your tickcounter and a MUD's tickcounter. The MUD's tick size isn't constant (the server may lose a few seconds while starting a new tick, or due to network lag, it might lose or gain a few seconds). So, it's best to synchronise your tick counter from time to time. A good place to do it is on events, arriving when a MUD starts a new tick ("You are hungry" message, messages about day start/end, etc.).

See also: #tick, #tickon, #tickoff, #ticksize
