#tabadd

Syntax: #tabadd word

This command adds word to the word extender list. Every time you press TAB key JMC attempts to complete unfinished word in the commandline to the words from this list.

Sample: #tabadd waterskin;#tabadd wellington

Every time you type w, then press TAB JMC will try to finish word, first time to “waterskin” word, next time to “wellington” word, next time to “w” again.

 

Also, you able to edit words list in the “Keywords” dialog.

See also: #tabdel
